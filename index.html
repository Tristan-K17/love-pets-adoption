About Us

At Loved Pets Animal Shelter, we believe every pet deserves a loving forever home. We rescue animals, provide them with veterinary care, and match them with families who will provide a lifetime of love and care. Our goal is to create a compassionate community where animals and people can thrive together.

Founded in 2010, our shelter has helped over 1,000 animals find their new homes. We are dedicated to giving each animal the best care possible until they find their perfect match. We also offer volunteer and donation opportunities, as we believe everyone has a role in helping our furry friends.
